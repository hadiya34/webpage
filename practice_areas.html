Green Card

Do you intend to establish a long-term relationship with the United States? You can live and work anywhere in the United States with an adjustment of status to lawful permanent resident, also known as a "Green Card holder," and you can eventually petition for citizenship. A close familial link with a citizen or permanent resident of the United States, employment-based green cards, and humanitarian green cards granted to those with asylum and refugee status or who have been victims of crime are the most popular types of green cards.
